Insomniac

How to sleep
admist the symphony?
when the twinkle stars
and the moon

How to sleep
admist the starlight-sun
and the warmth
with diamond tessellate leaves above

When whitecaps laugh
and the songbirds join
and the wheat a-waves
under rolling white puffs

the orange striped kit-cat naps
in the windowsill rays
ignoring the jabber-chatter
nearby

When the high flying falcon
soaring, gliding, flying, diving
all the while
wind dancer

Human,
I am not sure why you sleep
when the whole world rest
does not
